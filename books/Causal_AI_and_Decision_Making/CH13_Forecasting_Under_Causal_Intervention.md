# Decisions Under Uncertainty

Decision-making is fundamentally about making choices when the future is uncertain. This chapter covers the methods for quantifying, calibrating, and acting on uncertainty, including probabilistic forecasting, sensitivity analysis, and robustness evaluation. These techniques help decision-makers understand the range of possible outcomes and prepare strategies that work across different scenarios.
